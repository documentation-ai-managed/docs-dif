## Overview

Set up a digital wallet to manage funds within Dif.

## Steps
<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to Dif.tech">
    Access your dashboard at `https://dif.tech/dashboard`
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Wallets">
    Select "Wallets" from the sidebar menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Create Wallet'">
    Enter a name for your new wallet. Choose currency if prompted.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Creation">
    Review details, then click "Confirm". Your wallet is ready to use!
  </Step>
</Steps>
