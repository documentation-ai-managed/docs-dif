---
title: Cannot Link Bank Account
description: Fix issues when linking a bank account on Dif.
---

## Symptoms

- Bank account linking fails
- Error: "Connection to bank unavailable"

## Causes
- Incomplete KYC verification
- Incorrect bank credentials
- Bank system downtime

## Quick Fix
<Steps>
  <Step title="Check KYC status">
    Log into Dif, go to Settings > Profile, and ensure KYC shows as "Verified".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Re-enter credentials">
    Double-check your bank login details for typos.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Try later">
    If the issue persists, your bank may be temporarily unavailable. Wait and try again after 30 minutes.
  </Step>
</Steps>
