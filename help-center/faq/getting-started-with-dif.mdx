## Overview

Sign up and set up your digital banking account with Dif in minutes.

## Steps

1. Visit `https://dif.tech/register`
2. Enter email and set a secure password
3. Verify your email address
4. Complete KYC verification (upload ID)
5. Log in to your new Dif dashboard

You're ready to explore our fintech services!
